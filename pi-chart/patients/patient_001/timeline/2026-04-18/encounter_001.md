# Encounter 001 — pneumonia admission

## Reason for admission
Three-day history of productive cough with subjective fevers, now with
progressive shortness of breath and reduced exercise tolerance. Presented to
ED with SpO2 88% on room air, improved to 94% on 2L NC.

## Admission impression
Community-acquired pneumonia with left lower lobe consolidation on CXR,
requiring supplemental oxygen. Admit to medicine for IV antibiotics and
respiratory support.

## Initial plan
- Levofloxacin + azithromycin (selected to avoid beta-lactam given penicillin
  anaphylaxis; macrolide coverage for atypicals)
- Supplemental O2 via nasal cannula, titrate to SpO2 >= 92%
- Serial vitals with continuous SpO2 monitoring
- CBC, BMP, blood cultures x2, lactate, procalcitonin on admission
- Repeat CXR in 48h if not improving
- Deep vein thrombosis prophylaxis

## Disposition
Pending clinical trajectory.
