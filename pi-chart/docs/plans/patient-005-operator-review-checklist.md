## Scenario realism questions

1. Does POD0/POD1 timing make sense for hip-fracture repair, PACU-to-floor transfer, PT evaluation, and day-to-night safety handoff?
2. Is the pain/sedation balance realistic: low-dose opioid use for therapy-limiting pain without overclaiming medication-induced delirium?
3. Is the delirium/fall-risk evidence clinically credible: CAM-positive fluctuating attention, orthostatic dizziness, unsafe stand attempt, and two-person assist requirement?
4. Are baseline function and orientation supports visible enough: cane use, daughter support, glasses, hearing aids, and baseline oriented x4?
5. Are fall precautions appropriate and specific enough: bed/chair alarms, low bed, toileting schedule, assisted transfers, reorientation, and provider call criteria?
6. Do labs and vitals appropriately support safety context without pretending to explain all delirium risk?
7. Does the handoff and night reassessment preserve the same charted fact (`evt-005-0018`) across assessment, evidence, open loop, note, family communication, and next-shift plan?
